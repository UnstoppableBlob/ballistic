# Ballistic

## What this is

This is a twin-stick local 2d dodgeball game in development. Currently, only the character has been implemented, with the ability to teleport, shoot, and slow down time. This game is controller-only.

## How to play

Itch.io link: https://suspiciousitem.itch.io/ballistic

To play the game from the Itch.io link, here are the controls: left joystick to move, right joystick to aim. The right trigger will shoot, and if you aren't aiming, it will shoot in the direction of your movement. The right bumper will slow down time, and the left bumper will let you teleport. While you press the left bumper, you won't be able to move, and you can you the left joystick to control where you will be teleported when you let go of the button. There is a white dot that helps you with aiming and teleporting.
